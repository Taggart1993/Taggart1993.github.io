Metro 2034 Assignment

Myles Manship, 4/1/2022, Period 2 ELA, B. Habecker


Metro 2034 is a novel by Russian author Dmitry Glukhovsky (sequel to his previous work Metro 2033); set in a post-nuclear universe,
where the scattered remnants of humanity live pitiful, short lives underground.

Metro 2034 follows three protagonists: Hunter, a grizzly ex-soldier with esoteric intentions; Sasha, a young girl who is unsure of what to do in life; and Homer, an old man who simply
wants to finish his great masterpiece poem before he dies.

Unfortunately for our trio, a plague is breaking out near Rimskaya, and fate seems to have decided they should be the ones to stop it.
